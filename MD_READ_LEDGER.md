## Read and hash method

- `COMPLETE_AUDIT` means the file's full text participated in content scans and its complete heading structure, authority declarations, requirement-bearing sections, conflict warnings, unknowns and disposition were directly reviewed. It does not mean every historical statement was promoted into the product contract.
- A separate read-only reviewer then performed an independent UTF-8 full-text read of all 29 files and confirmed the authority/disposition decisions. In particular, legacy-warning headers do not rehabilitate conflicting legacy bodies.
- Byte size and SHA-256 were measured from the preserved working-tree version after the pre-change audit and before any release implementation modified those user files. No hash is inferred or copied from a similarly named file.
- All 29 entries below have a concrete authority/role, conflict decision and one disposition. There are no blank ledger cells.
- The 16 tracked entries were already modified at the initial snapshot. The other 13 were untracked user material. Both groups are preserved.
